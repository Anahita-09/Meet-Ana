About

Hey! I'm Anahita Mayekar. My goal is to become a computer engineer, providing technological support to people. I enjoy how hardware and software unite. I learn programming languages like Java and Python. I utilize tools like Figma and SQL. Someday, I will add to that dialogue, but I know technical skill alone doesn't work without human connection. According to Paul J. Meyer, "Communication - the human connection - is the key to personal and career success." This thought process shifted my mindset from being a person in a new country to a determined leader. Through each presentation, each partnership, and each conversation, I form connections between people and ideas.
