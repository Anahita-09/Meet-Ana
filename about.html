About

I'm Anahita Mayekar — a tech enthusiast and passionate advocate for STEM education for youth. I've had the opportunity to engage with panels led by industry leaders from top tech companies, and I also volunteer as a tutor in STEM and language arts. Both experiences have strengthened my belief that computer science education for young people is essential for the future. I'm a developer with hands-on experience in Python, Java, p5.js, and Web/AI development. When I'm not coding or studying, you'll probably find me playing guitar or indulging in peri peri cheese fries.
